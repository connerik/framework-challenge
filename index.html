MI 349 Film Festival

This is not a real film. But let's pretend that it is. The first film being shown at this film festival is a fantasy movie about web design. Fairies making websites or something like that.

The second film being screened at the festival is a horror movie about Figma. The main protagonist made a really awesome idea for a website in Figma but then his idea came to life and started to attack him...scary...

The third and final movie to be shown at the film festival is a thriller feature. This movie follows a web designer who has been challenged by an anonymous group to create a website based on a randomized challenge prompt, otherwise she loses all of the money in her bank.

Where: 349 Web Design Street
